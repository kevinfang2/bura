About Us

Providing 1:1 access to digital devices for creditless peri-rural and rural populations in Africa

In Ghana, unemployment among the youth (persons aged 15-35) is at 12.1%. Although Digital Accessibility is essential for education and financial inclusion, many Ghanaians are not connected.

We operate digital hubs educate local workers on crypto and play-to-earn games in underserved regions of Ghana

Problem

Pressing need for tech infrastructure and awareness for West Africa Crypto Adoption

Facing volatile currencies and geopolitical risk, Africans stand to greatly benefit from crypto and defi. However, due to a lack of digital devices and ICT training, generations of Africans in peri-rural and rural areas are unable to access such technologies.


Play-to-earn games represent an opportunity to educate and onboard populations into the crypto ecosystem, but have yet to be prevalent in Western Africa due to low rates of ownership of digital devices.

Solution

Introducing crypto adoption through play-to-earn games to catapault economic opportunity

We operate digital hubs educate local workers on crypto and play-to-earn games in underserved regions of Ghana.


Once the training is complete, local workers are onboarded into our guild


We then directly distribute electronic devices through a lease-to-own model to members of our guild, in exchange for a split of their earnings

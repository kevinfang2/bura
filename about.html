Problem

Bura is providing 1:1 access to digital devices for creditless peri-rural and rural populations in West Africa

In Ghana, unemployment among the youth (persons aged 15-35) is at 12.1%, which doesn't take into account:

• An additional 28% of youth are out of the labor force as discouraged workers

• Many youth are forced to join the private informal sector, which currently accounts for 84.8% of the working population

Solution

We operate digital hubs educate local workers on crypto and play-to-earn games in underserved regions of Ghana.

Once the training is complete, local workers are onboarded into our guild

We then directly distribute electronic devices through a lease-to-own model to members of our guild, in exchange for a split of their earnings
